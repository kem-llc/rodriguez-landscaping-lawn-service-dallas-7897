Landscape Design & Installation

Transform your property with custom landscape designs featuring native plants, flower beds, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our landscape design service brings your vision to life. We start with a consultation to understand your style, needs, and budget. Then we create a detailed plan with plant selections, hardscape elements, and layout. Our installation team handles everything from grading and planting to mulch and decorative stone. We specialize in drought-tolerant xeriscaping and colorful perennial gardens that thrive in Dallas.

What's included

  • Personalized designs that reflect your taste
  • Expert plant selection for long-lasting beauty
  • Professional installation with attention to detail
  • Enhance curb appeal and property value

How it works

1

Consultation

We meet on-site to discuss your ideas, measure the space, and review your goals.

2

Design & Approval

We create a 2D or 3D design, present it to you, and refine until perfect.

3

Installation

Our crew installs plants, beds, edging, and any hardscape features with precision.
