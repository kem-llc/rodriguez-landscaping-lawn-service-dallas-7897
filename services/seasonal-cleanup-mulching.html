Seasonal Cleanup & Mulching

Spring and fall cleanups, leaf removal, and fresh mulch application.

Overview

Keep your yard tidy through the seasons with our comprehensive cleanup services. We remove leaves, debris, and dead plants, then trim back overgrowth. Our mulching service suppresses weeds, retains moisture, and gives beds a polished look. We use premium hardwood or colored mulch.

What's included

  • Prepares your yard for each season
  • Reduces weed growth
  • Improves soil health
  • Enhances curb appeal instantly
